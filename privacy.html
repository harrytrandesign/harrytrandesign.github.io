Privacy policy

How we handle your information

Effective Date: October 01, 2017

Welcome to Thought Experience Labs (known as "we", "us", "our"). Thought Experience Labs is a mobile development studio that creates applications for our users ("you") to share your content.

Our Privacy Policy briefly explains how we and some of the companies we work with collect, use, share, and protect information within our service, and your choices about the collection and use of your information. By using our service you understand and agree that we are providing you with a platform to share photos and generate content ("Content") that will be shared publicly. This means other users will be able to view, comment, and share content that you made publicly available.

Our Policy applies to all visitors, users, and others who access the service.

Information That We Collect:

We collect the following types of information.

Your username, password, email address when you register for a Thought Experience Labs account. We authenticate your information with Google+ services. Any profile information that you provide for your user profile such as first and last name, as well as a profile picture. User generated content, such as photos and comments, that is posted to the service.

We use third party analytic tools to help us measure traffic and views and usage trends. This information is collected and aggregated with information from other users without being able to identify individual users.

When you use our service our servers automatically record certain log file information, such as your device identifier, and internet protocol address (IP Address). A mobile device identifier can uniquely identify your mobile device such as a mobile phone or tablet. Your content, photos and comments, are uniquely tied to your username and also device identifier information. We also store the time that the content was submitted.

Our advertisers may use cookies or similar technology to place and track ad performance and advertisement traffic as well as record clicks and display ads.

Your content may be accessed via our mobile application ("app") or there will be a physical web address that can be used to view the content.

How We Use Your Information:

We may use your information that we receive to help you efficiently access your information after you sign in. Remember your information so you don't have to sign in again during future visits to the service. Provide personalized content and information to you and others, including online ads and other forms of marketing. Provide, improve, and test our services as well as help develop new products and features. Diagnose technical problems.

Sharing Your Information:

We will not rent or sell your information to third parties without your consent except as noted in this policy.

We may share your user generated content and your information with our affiliates as well as with advertisers. Our affiliates and advertisers will be given access to your information as reasonably necessary to provide you with services under reasonable confidentiality terms. Our advertisers may also see your data via cookies which will allow them to deliver targeted advertisements that will be of more interest to you.

Any information or content that you voluntarily post to our service will become available to the public, content posted to our service may be re-shared by others.

We may access, preserve and share your information in response to a legal request if we have a good faith belief that the law requires us to do so. This may include responding to legal requests from jurisdictions outside of the United States where we have a good faith belief that the response is required by law in that jurisdiction, affects users in that jurisdiction, and is consistent with internationally recognized standards.

Storing Your Information:

Your information that is collected through our service may be stored in the United States or anywhere our affiliates maintain facilities. Information may be transferred across borders.

After deleting your account or having your account terminated we will keep your information on our servers for a reasonable time, all user generated content may still remain on the service unless requested to be removed with permission from the user.

Children's Privacy:

Thought Experience Labs does not knowingly collect or solicit information from any persons under the age of 13, or knowingly allow such persons to register for our service. Our services and its content are not directed towards children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under the age of 13 without their parent\'s consent we will delete their information and content as quickly as possible. If you believe we have information from a child under 13 please contact us immediately.
